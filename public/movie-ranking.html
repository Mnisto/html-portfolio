The Best Movies According to Sanele

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Count of Monte Carlo

The Count of Monte Cristo" is a classic novel written by French author Alexandre Dumas. It was first serialized between 1844 and 1846 and has since become one of the most widely read and beloved works of literature. The novel is known for its thrilling plot, intricate characters, and themes of revenge, justice, and redemption.

The Prestige

"The Prestige" delves into themes of rivalry, obsession, sacrifice, and the lengths to which people will go to achieve their goals. The film explores the complexities of human nature and the blurred lines between illusion and reality.

Law abiding citizen

The main theme of "The Law Abiding Citizen" is the concept of justice and the moral ambiguity surrounding it. The film explores the idea of what happens when the justice system fails to deliver the appropriate punishment for heinous crimes and how individuals might take matters into their own hands when they feel betrayed by the legal system.
